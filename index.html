About Me

I am Sepehr Asgarian, a Machine Learning researcher specializing in the development and optimization of Large Language Models (LLMs). Currently, I work as a Data Scientist at Klick Inc., a healthcare intelligence and technology company, where I develop and deploy AI-driven solutions for pharmaceutical and healthcare applications. My role involves building predictive models, implementing machine learning pipelines, and leveraging generative AI technologies to solve complex healthcare challenges.

I earned my MSc in Computer Science at Western University under the supervision of Professor Yalda Mohsenzadeh, specializing in advanced neural networks for brain-computer interfaces. My research pioneered novel deep learning architectures and contrastive learning frameworks to solve cross-domain generalization challenges in neural signal processing. Previously, I completed my BSc in Computer Engineering at Amirkabir University of Technology, exploring deep learning for anomaly detection and text analysis.

My expertise spans generative AI, large language models, and multimodal machine learning systems with applications across healthcare, multimedia, and enterprise solutions. I have extensive experience building production-ready AI systems, implementing advanced ML pipelines, and deploying models that drive business value. My work has been published in top-tier academic conferences and journals including NeurIPS, AAAI, and IEEE Transactions.
